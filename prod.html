Kevin Feige

American film and television producer who has been the president of Marvel Studios and the primary producer of the Marvel Cinematic Universe franchise since 2007.

He was nominated for a Producers Guild of America Award for Best Theatrical Motion Picture and Best Picture at the Academy Awards for his work on Black Panther.

Stan Lee

Also known as Stan the Man, born Stanley Martin Lieber, was one of the first and most prolific writers / creators of the Marvel Universe.

He played small parts in every Marvel Cinematic Universe film and TV series with Avengers: Endgame serving as his final cameo appearance.

Jon Favreau

Jonathan Kolia "Jon" Favreau directed Iron Man and Iron Man 2.

He was nominated for a Scream Award for Best Director for his work on Iron Man.

Avi Arad

The former head of Marvel Studios.

He was a producer on Iron Man and The Incredible Hulk and an executive producer on Spider-Man.

Amy Pascal

Amy Beth Pascal was a producer on Spider-Man: Homecoming, Spider-Man: Far From Home, and Spider-Man: No Way Home, and will produce Spider-Man 4.

Amy Pascal previously worked with Kevin Feige

Stephen Broussard

Stephen Broussard is a VP of Production & Development at Marvel Studios.

He also portrayed Handsome Soldier in The Incredible Hulk.

Jonathan Schwartz

Jonathan Schwartz is a VP of Production & Development at Marvel Studios.
